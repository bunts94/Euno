Founded in May 2017, Euno is a social platform that allows individuals to build and develop an organizational network in order to create and maintain structure within their lives.

Regardless of whether you need to take and share notes, or keep track of your schedule via a calendar and to-do list, Euno empowers users with a versatile and user-friendly platform that satisfies such necessities. With this organizational network and ease of access to interconnected tools, Euno is the most efficient way to design your organizational framework and share with others.
